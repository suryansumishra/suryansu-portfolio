Tech-Tracks

Tech-Tracks is a performance tracking platform designed for academic institutions to monitor and evaluate faculty teaching effectiveness. It collects structured feedback from both students and academic authorities, analyzes teaching quality, and generates insightful reports. By combining feedback analytics with an easy-to-use dashboard, TechTrack helps institutions ensure teaching standards, support faculty development, and enhance the overall learning experience.

Certificate Sharing

Certificate Sharing Website is a digital platform that allows users to securely upload, manage, and share their academic, professional, or training certificates online. It provides unique shareable links for verification, ensuring authenticity and easy access. The platform reduces paperwork, prevents certificate forgery, and helps employers or institutions quickly validate credentials.
